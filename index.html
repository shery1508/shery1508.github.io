PHILOSOPHY

Word “Philosophy” comes from Greek words meaning “love of wisdom.” Philosophy uses the tools of logic and reason to analyze the ways in which humans experience the world. It teaches critical thinking, close reading, clear writing, and logical analysis; it uses these to understand the language we use to describe the world, and our place within it. Different areas of philosophy are distinguished by the questions they ask. Folowing are the some main banches of philosophy

  1. Axiology
  2. Metaphysics
  3. Epistemology
  4. Ethics
  5. Political Philosophy
  6. Aesthetics
  7. Logic

"Philosophy is a science which discovers the real nature of supernatural elements"
 According to Aristotle

"He who has a taste for every sort of knowledge and who is curious to learn and is never satisfied may be justly termed as a philosopher."
According to Plato
